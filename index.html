About Me

I love the outdoors and being active. I paraglide in the Lake District, hike regularly, paddleboard, and have recently started a van conversion project to better facilitate these weekend adventures! I also enjoy running and tennis, I recently completed my first marathon (just!) and love travelling off the beaten track.

I would describe myself as a technologist and a generalist; I regularly teach myself different technologies or languages for better understanding. I'm reasonably proficient in html, css, a little Javascript, Ruby and the Ruby on Rails Framework, and Django. I'm also currently finishing building a Sustainability Management platform for SMEs using the low-code tool 'Bubble'.

I am very interested and engaged in business innovation, particularly within start-ups and SMEs. I like to make small investments in young businesses with a focus on sustainability.
